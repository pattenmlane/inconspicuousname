## Wall layer (two rungs per side)

**278076** bid persistence order: −8 → **−11 (40.4%)** → **−10 (39.0%)**  
**248329** bid persistence order: −8 → **−10 (42.5%)** → **−11 (36.8%)**

So the **relative frequency of the two deep bid rungs swaps** between days (which rung appears more often), but both days still show **two** mass modes near **−10 / −11** and **+10 / +11** with **~38–42%** persistence each.
