1) One-sample t (mean vs 0)

     t = mean(x) / (sd(x) / sqrt(n))   with sample sd, ddof=1
   Reported as t_same_sym_mean0, t_extract_mean0, t_hydro_mean0 in 01_participant_fwd_summary.csv
   and t_fwd20_mean0 in 02_stratified_* and 08_aggressor_*.

2) Bootstrap percentile CI for mean (95%, B replicates)
   Resample rows of x with replacement, B=400 for participant summary (01_),
   B=250 for stratified top-80 (02_) and aggressor bucket (08_) to limit runtime.
   Alpha=0.05 → 2.5% and 97.5% percentiles of bootstrap means.
   Random seed fixed per bucket for reproducibility.

   Reported alongside mean for same_sym, extract, hydro in 01_; median_fwd20 in 02_ and 08_.
