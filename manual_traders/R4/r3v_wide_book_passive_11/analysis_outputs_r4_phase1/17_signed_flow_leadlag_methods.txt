Phase 1 — signed flow vs extract forward (15_signed_flow_leadlag_vs_extract_fwd20.csv)

Per (day, timestamp, symbol): net_signed_qty = sum over trade rows of
  +quantity if aggressor=buy_aggr (touch buy),
  -quantity if aggressor=sell_aggr (touch sell),
   0 if mid_passive or unknown.

Merged with extract_fwd_20 = mid(t+20 rows) - mid(t) on VELVETFRUIT_EXTRACT at same (day, timestamp).

For each day, flow_symbol in {EXTRACT, HYDROGEL_PACK, VEV_5300, VEV_5200, VEV_4000},
and lag L in {0,1,2,3,5} **trade-event rows** (not wall-clock):
  correlate( net_signed_qty shifted by L events within that (day, symbol) series,
             extract_fwd_20 )
Only rows where both sides finite; require n >= 25 after dropna.

Interpretation: L>0 means signed flow on the named product **precedes** the extract
forward measured at the same wall timestamp by L print events on that product —
a discrete-time lead–lag proxy (Phase 1 graph / structural flow).
