Phase 1 bullet 2 — stratified baseline cells (18_, 19_, 20_)

Cells: groupby (buyer, seller, symbol, spread_q) on merged trade×L1 rows,
same-symbol fwd_mid_20 as elsewhere.

Minimum count per cell: 25 prints.

Residual_vs_spread_stratum = cell_mean - global_mean_same_spread_q
where global_mean_same_spread_q is the **unconditional** mean of fwd_mid_20
over **all** trades in that spread_q tertile (tight/mid/wide), same K=20.

Interpretation: positive residual means this (pair, symbol) bucket beats
the typical print in the same book-width regime — closer to "(buyer,seller,symbol,regime)"
baseline than the pooled 03_/04_ tables.
