This folder is the **canonical winding-DTE** implementation: `t_years_effective` decreases
intraday (~one calendar day over the session) for historical CSV days 0–2 (DTE 8/7/6 at open).

A symlinked view of the same code lives under `../wind_down/combined_analysis/`.

For **calendar T only** (no intraday wind), use `../no_wind_down/combined_analysis/`.
