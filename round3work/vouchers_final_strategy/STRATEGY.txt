# Voucher + extract — tight two-leg gate (Round 3)

## Thesis (reconstructed in-repo)

When **VEV_5200** and **VEV_5300** both have top-of-book spread **ask1 − bid1 ≤ 2** (same tick units as
`Prosperity4Data/ROUND_3` tapes) at the **same timestamp**, the joint book is a **risk-on / tradable
microstructure** regime: short-horizon **VELVETFRUIT_EXTRACT** mid behavior is more favorable than when
either book is wide. Use this as a **regime filter** and/or to **size** extract + VEV activity.

- **Sonic-style:** edge statistics should be **gated** on voucher BBO state; widening past the threshold =
  different regime.
- **inclineGod-style:** use **per-contract spreads** and **joint** spread conditions, not mids alone.
